Miso Soup


Description

Ingredients:

  • Water
  • Dashi
  • Tofu
  • Miso
  • Seaweed
  • Green Onion

Steps:

  1. Gather all the ingredients
  2. Cut the onion into thin rounds
  3. Add the water to the Dashi
  4. Add the dashi to the saucepan and simmer
  5. Add the miso and stir
  6. Cut the tofu into cubes and add to the miso soup
  7. Add teh dried wakame seaweed and reheat the miso soup but don't boil
  8. Serve immediately.
