Nikujaga


Description

Ingredients:

  • Vegetables (potatoes, carrots, onions)
  • Sliced pork
  • Soy sauce, sake, sugar, mirin, dashi
  • Water

Steps:

  1. Cut the vegetables
  2. Cook and stir beef
  3. When the beef turned brown, add vegetables
  4. Add sugar and water
  5. Add soy sauce, sake, mirin and dashi and then simmer for 20-30 minutes
