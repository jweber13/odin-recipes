Onigiri


Description

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Furikake
  • Nori


Steps:

  1. Cook the rice, then put into mix bowl
  2. Add the furikake and mix evenly
  3. Separate rice into equal portions
  4. Wet your hands with water and rub together with salt
  5. In your hands, make an indent on the rice
  6. Place ingredients inside and fold rice over
  7. Sqeeze into a triangle shape
  8. Place a slice of nori at the bottom, and fold upwards towards the middle
